# SVACC Full-Stack Content Team

- **Creative Producer**: Oversees the entire content project, ensures vision and deadlines are met
- **Story Architect**: Designs the narrative structure, flow, and pacing
- **Visual Designer**: Creates graphics, layouts, and visual assets
- **Scriptwriter**: Writes scripts, chapters, or blog posts
- **Researcher**: Gathers data, trends, and inspiration
- **Editor**: Refines drafts, ensures clarity and consistency
- **Voice Artist**: Records narration or voiceovers
- **Video Editor**: Assembles and edits video content
- **QA Reviewer**: Checks quality, accuracy, and alignment
- **Distribution Manager**: Publishes and promotes content
